Welcome to Aditya Degree College, Srikakulam

Established in 1998, Aditya Degree College has been a leading institution in providing quality education in Srikakulam.

About Us

Aditya Degree College is affiliated to Andhra University and has been accredited by NAAC with 'A' grade.

  • Vision: To provide quality education and empower students to become responsible citizens.
  • Mission: To provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

Courses Offered

  • B.Sc. (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry)
  • B.Com. (General, Computers)
  • BA (History, Economics, Politics)
  • M.Sc. (Organic Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry)
  • M.Com. (General, Financial Management)

Facilities

  • Library with over 10,000 books and journals
  • Computer lab with latest software and hardware
  • Science labs with modern equipment
  • Hostel facilities for boys and girls
  • Playground and sports facilities
